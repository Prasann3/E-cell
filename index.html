About The Event

In a startup nothing happens unless you make it happen. Startup camp promotes entrepreneurship among students and also provides a start-up initiative activity amongst the growing startups in and around Chhattisgarh. Several high scale and low scale startups in and around Chhattisgarh are invited and provided with an opportunity to publicize their products/services and grab some interns for their companies. The investors attend the pitches of the startups and on recognizing a potential startup, provide seed funding to those startups.
